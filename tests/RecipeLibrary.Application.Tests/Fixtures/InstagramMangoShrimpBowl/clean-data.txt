Day 4/30 Healthy Bowls - Mango Shrimp Fiesta Bowl 🥭🍤

It is bright, fresh, takes 15 minutes, and honestly might be my favourite one yet.

RECIPE (serves 2):

Ingredients:
• 1 cup basmati rice
• 10 oz raw shrimp (peeled, deveined)
• 1/2 tsp cumin
• 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
• 1/2 tsp chili 
• 1/4 tsp oregano
• 1/4 tsp garlic powder
• Salt and pepper
• 1 ripe mango, diced
• 1/2 red onion, diced
• 1 tbsp cilantro, chopped
• 1/4 jalapeño, sliced
• 1/4 avocado, diced
• 1/4 cup fresh cilantro (for rice)
• Juice of 1 lime
• Lime wedges for serving

Steps:
1. Cook jasmine rice. While it’s still hot, stir in chopped cilantro and fresh lime juice. This is the move that makes the whole bowl.
2. Season shrimp with cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and salt. Sear in a hot pan, 2 minutes per side.
3. Make mango salsa: combine diced mango, red onion, cilantro, and half the jalapeño slices. Squeeze lime over it.
4. Build your bowls: cilantro lime rice on the bottom, shrimp, mango salsa, diced avocado, remaining jalapeño slices. Serve with lime wedges.
